**Premise**

> *Without skills, you\'re only using about 10% of what Cowork can do. I personally use over 50 skills every single day to run an \$80K/mo business with no employees.*

This video gives away 15 skills as one bundled plugin. Install once → all skills ready. The other 35 the host uses are in his Skool community.

**How to Install the Bundled Plugin**

1.  Download the .zip file (link in description). DO NOT open the zip --- Cowork needs zip format.

2.  Save zip to desktop.

3.  Open Claude Cowork → Customize → Plugins → Add Plugin.

4.  Click Upload Plugin.

5.  Find the zip on desktop. Click Upload.

6.  All 15 skills appear in plugin folder.

7.  Optional: click Customize to tailor plugin to YOUR business.

**Skill 1 --- Slide Deck Builder**

Generates branded slide decks. The video\'s own slideshow was made with this skill. Trigger via {backslash}slide-deck-builder OR natural language: \'Create a slideshow for X.\' Has design system, visual components, slide text rules, navigation, all preconfigured.

**Skill 2 --- Customize Skill**

Customizes any of the other 14 skills to your business context, branding, design preferences. Trigger {backslash}customize. Asks targeted questions: which skill, what\'s most important, branding colors, visual vibe (e.g., Swiss style design). Outputs a customized version.

**Skill 3 --- Difficult Conversation Prep**

Trigger: \'Prep me for a difficult conversation.\' Asks follow-up questions: type (work/professional/personal), core issue (performance/relationship/etc), main concern, who you\'re talking to.

**Output Includes**

-   Mindset framing --- \'this isn\'t a punishment conversation, this is being a good manager.\'

-   Suggested timing (early in week, private setting).

-   What NOT to do.

-   Opening lines.

-   Key points to make.

**Skill 4 --- Receipt Categorizer**

Drop receipts into folder. Skill reads, categorizes spending, generates spreadsheet AND expense dashboard. Replaces accountant/bookkeeper for monthly expense tracking.

**Pair With Scheduled Task**

Most useful pattern: turn this into a scheduled task. Run every Friday 5 PM at end of week. From Cowork: click Schedule → \'I want this run every Friday at 5 PM.\' Creates the schedule.

**Skill 5 --- Explainer Graphic Skill (Top 5)**

Builds infographic HTML page with real-world analogies for any concept. Example used: \'Create explainer about how bridges get built over water.\' Output: full animated HTML site. \'Don\'t build bridge across water --- build it from the bottom. Like building fence around a pond.\' Survey site → cofferdam → drill to bedrock.

Best for research, school, learning, when you need bite-sized comprehension.

**Skill 6 --- Email Drafter**

Cold outreach, follow-ups, negotiations. Connect to Gmail → can pull all emails for the day, summarize, draft responses.

**Skill 7 --- Visual Page Builder**

Builds interactive HTML site with sections you click through, animations, smooth transitions. Useful for community resources, documentation. Example: \'how large language models actually work\' --- generated full educational site.

**Skill 8 --- Invoice Generator**

Generates invoices in your preferred format from one-line description. Example: \'Generate invoice for Bob, AI contracting work, \$75K. Come up with rest of details.\' Output: PDF invoice with services breakdown, rates, invoice number, payment terms --- fully formatted.

**Skill 9 --- Contract Reviewer**

Paste any contract OR have it scour email for one. Adds red flags (clauses that hurt you), missing terms, what to negotiate. Covers freelance contractor agreements, NDAs, employment contracts.

**Output Markdown**

-   Short version.

-   Key terms.

-   Specific red flags.

-   Yellow flags.

-   What\'s missing.

-   Bottom line --- should you sign?

-   Specific things to negotiate.

**Skill 10 --- Workflow Visualizer**

Visualizes how complex Cowork systems work --- interactive site with phases, commands, integrations, revenue engines.

Host\'s example: visualizes his entire Claude Code/Cowork content business OS. Click each phase → see commands, skills, integrations, what triggers what. Daily triggers (morning briefing, email briefing, competitor intel), phase 2 ideation/research, etc.

**Skill 11 --- Morning Briefing (Author\'s Favorite)**

Scheduled task running every morning at 7-8 AM. Sends:

-   Calendar overview for the day.

-   Industry news.

-   Email inbox triage with urgent emails.

-   Suggested replies you can use.

-   Sent to Slack AND in HTML dashboard.

**Pairing Skills With Connectors**

Customize → Connectors. Cowork has \~50-70 native connectors. Author has Gmail, Calendar, Notion, Slack, Canva, HubSpot.

**Skill 12 --- Budget Planner**

Personal or business. Asks: time period, goal, income, fixed expenses, savings goal.

**Output**

-   Interactive financial dashboard.

-   Income, expenses, savings per month.

-   Saving rate as percentage.

-   Modify income live → updates everything (rate, percent saved, savings after 12 months).

-   Visualize spending categories.

**Skill 13 --- Quick Research**

Trigger: \'Quick research \[topic\].\' Outputs markdown with summary, how it works, key features, requirements/availability, pricing, pros/cons, bottom line, all resources used.

Author uses this for YouTube video research and AI product release breakdowns.

**Skill 14 --- Animated Website Generator**

Built host\'s actual landing page from one prompt. Animated text, smooth panning, product reveal. Detailed standalone video on host\'s channel: \'How to build \$10K+ animated websites with Cowork.\'

**Skill 15 --- Learning Path Generator**

Week-by-week curriculum with resources, milestones, projects.

Example: \'Learning how agentic workflows work.\' Asks questions about time commitment and starting level. Outputs phased plan with resources (Anthropic docs, etc.).
