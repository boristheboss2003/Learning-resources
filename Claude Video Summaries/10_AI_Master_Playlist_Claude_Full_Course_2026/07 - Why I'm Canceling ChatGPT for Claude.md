**Premise**

OpenAI removed their ban on military use from terms of service and announced Pentagon partnership for cybersecurity tools. Anthropic was approached for the same deal --- they said no.

> *They walked away from what was likely millions in government contracts because they actually meant it when they said they care about AI safety and user privacy.*

The video frames this as a defining moment. The host argues users should evaluate where their conversation history goes, who can access it, and whether to switch tools accordingly.

**Why Anthropic Said No**

-   Anthropic founded by former OpenAI researchers who left over direction disagreements.

-   Backed by Amazon and Google but consistently chose user privacy over government contracts.

-   Different philosophy: helpfulness, harmlessness, honesty --- not helpfulness to defense contractors.

**Importing ChatGPT Memory Into Claude**

**Step 1 --- Open Claude Memory Settings**

1.  Profile picture (bottom left).

2.  Settings.

3.  Capabilities tab.

4.  Click \'Import memory from other AI providers.\'

5.  Click \'Start Import.\'

6.  Two fields appear; first contains an extraction prompt that pulls memories from ChatGPT.

7.  Copy this prompt.

**Step 2 --- Configure ChatGPT for Maximum Extraction**

8.  Profile picture in ChatGPT.

9.  Personalization tab.

10. Enable all three: Reference saved memories, Reference browser memories, Reference chat history.

11. Switch model from Auto to \'Thinking\'.

Why Thinking model: does many more cycles of searching account history, finds info regular model misses. In testing: 3x more memories extracted including details from months-old conversations.

**Step 3 --- Run Extraction**

12. Paste Claude\'s extraction prompt into ChatGPT.

13. Watch Thinking model go through reasoning cycles.

14. Output is a code block with all your information.

15. Click \'Copy\' in top right corner of code block.

**Step 4 --- Paste Into Claude**

16. Back in Claude memory import dialog.

17. Right-click in text field → paste.

18. Click \'Add to memory.\'

19. Claude formats memories into clean categories --- better organized than ChatGPT\'s.

20. Memories update automatically every night based on new conversations.

21. Click pen icon next to any memory to edit if you spot mistakes.

**Recreating Custom GPTs as Claude Projects**

22. In ChatGPT, open custom GPT.

23. Click GPT name at top → Edit GPT.

24. Copy everything from the Instructions section (core logic).

25. In Claude, create a new project with same name.

26. Project Settings → paste instructions into Custom Instructions field.

27. Same context and behavior rules now in Claude.

**Why Projects Are Better**

-   Up to 200,000 tokens of context retained permanently.

-   All project documents, instructions, conversation history persist.

-   After \~12 hours, Claude generates project-specific memories.

**Complete ChatGPT Data Export**

28. Settings → Data Controls → Export Data.

29. Email arrives with zip file containing every conversation.

**Personalized Style Guide**

While waiting for export, ask ChatGPT:

> *Based on our conversation history, create a detailed guide of my communication preferences, common tasks, and optimal ways to assist me.*

Result: instruction set tailored to your actual usage patterns. Add to Claude\'s custom instructions.

**Deleting ChatGPT Account (Optional)**

-   Settings → Data Controls → bottom of page → Delete Account.

-   Type \'delete\' to confirm.

-   Cancel active subscriptions BEFORE --- otherwise you\'ll still get charged.

-   30-day deletion window --- recoverable during this time.

-   After 30 days OpenAI claims they delete data permanently.

-   Privacy policy is intentionally vague about what data persists for legal obligations.

**Real-World Comparison Tests**

**Test 1 --- Decline Meeting Email**

-   ChatGPT: generic corporate template, sounds like handbook.

-   Claude: asks clarifying questions about context, relationship, desired outcome → produces something that sounds human.

**Test 2 --- Blog Post Introductions**

-   ChatGPT: defaults to overused opening phrases (\'In today\'s rapidly evolving landscape\').

-   Claude: hooks that make you want to keep reading.

**Test 3 --- Research**

-   ChatGPT: surface-level answers. \'Like asking the student who only read the summary.\'

-   Claude: identifies when sources disagree. Points out gaps. Tells you what it doesn\'t know instead of filling gaps with plausible-sounding generalizations.

**Test 4 --- Long Creative Projects**

-   ChatGPT: forgets character details. Loses plot threads in 3 exchanges.

-   Claude: maintains continuity through dozens of iterations. Remembers subtle details from original concept.

**Test 5 --- Trip Planning With Constraints**

Budget, dietary, weather, multiple people\'s interests.

-   ChatGPT: standard itinerary.

-   Claude: detailed itinerary with realistic timing. Generates interactive map per day showing locations, walking routes, travel times. When asked to add constraint (\'friend just became vegetarian, another afraid of heights\'): Claude restructured plan to ensure everyone enjoyed it, suggested splitting group for certain activities.

**Test 6 --- Python Web Scraper**

-   ChatGPT: basic scraper for one site.

-   Claude: modular system, multiple sites, rate limiting, error recovery, suggested rotating proxies for larger scale.
