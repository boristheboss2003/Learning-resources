**Premise**

20 hours testing all three across 11 categories. Each category scored out of 10 based on quality, accuracy, real-world usability.

**Final Scoreboard**

  ---------- ----------------- ---------------------------------------------
  **Tool**   **Total / 110**   **Verdict**
  ChatGPT    88                Overall winner --- balanced general purpose
  Claude     84                Best for brand voice, nuanced writing
  Gemini     78                Best if your team lives in Google Workspace
  ---------- ----------------- ---------------------------------------------

**Category 1 --- Text Generation**

  ---------- ----------- -----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  **Tool**   **Score**   **Notes**
  ChatGPT    8/10        Solid foundation, professional polish, balance corporate/conversational
  Gemini     6/10        Stiff. \'Cutting-edge technology\' generic phrases. Significant rewrites needed
  Claude     9/10        Persuasive --- understands not just what to say but why someone would care. Publication-ready
  ---------- ----------- -----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

**Category 2 --- Data Analysis**

  ---------- ----------- -----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  **Tool**   **Score**   **Notes**
  ChatGPT    9/10        Clear actionable analysis, identifies patterns, business insights
  Gemini     6/10        Surface level --- correct numbers, no depth on why or what next
  Claude     10/10       Most actionable --- contextualizes, identifies underlying patterns, strategic recommendations
  ---------- ----------- -----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

**Category 3 --- Web Search & Fact-Checking**

All three tied at 10/10 for raw factual accuracy. Differences: Gemini has slightly more detail via Google integration; ChatGPT highly cautious; Claude factual but readable.

**Category 4 --- Logical Reasoning**

All three around 6/10. Reasoning is the weak spot for all major models --- they walk through but conclusions don\'t always hold up. Human verification still required for strategic planning, decision-making, anything needing airtight logic.

**Category 5 --- Roleplay/Character Work**

  ---------- ----------- ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  **Tool**   **Score**   **Notes**
  ChatGPT    8/10        Decent but professional politeness creeps in even when character should be blunt
  Gemini     5/10        Softens everything into corporate speak. Even direct character feels PR-filtered
  Claude     9/10        Persona comes alive. Sharp when needed. Direct without apology. Stays locked through entire response
  ---------- ----------- ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

**Category 6 --- Image Generation**

Claude doesn\'t have native image generation --- analyzes only. ChatGPT vs Gemini:

-   ChatGPT: 7/10 --- polished visuals, vibrant colors, balanced compositions, reliable text rendering.

-   Gemini: 9/10 --- striking quality, crisp, vivid, dynamic compositions, more natural lighting.

**Category 7 --- Special Features (Ecosystem)**

  ---------- ----------- --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  **Tool**   **Score**   **Standout**
  ChatGPT    8/10        Custom GPTs, Canvas mode, advanced voice, Sora 2 (limited beta), Computer Use
  Gemini     9/10        Workspace integration at scale --- Gems in Docs/Sheets/Slides/Drive/Gmail. VO3 video. Live video mode
  Claude     8/10        Projects with team collaborative memory, Artifacts as live editor, Claude Code, Web search with citations. No native video gen
  ---------- ----------- --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  ------------------ ----------- ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

**Category 9 --- Voice Switching/Brand Voice**

  ---------- ----------- -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  **Tool**   **Score**   **Notes**
  ChatGPT    7/10        Decent. Softens edges, especially sharp/sarcastic voices. Tries to be safe
  Gemini     4/10        Defaults to formal even when instructed otherwise. Can give corporate language to sarcastic samples
  Claude     9/10        Nails style matching. Holds tone through edits. Sarcasm stays sarcastic. Memory weak --- keeps trimming when shortening
  ---------- ----------- -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

**Category 10 --- Research**

  ---------- ----------- ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  **Tool**   **Score**   **Strength**

  Gemini     9/10        Deep Research mode + Search Grounding. Tightest Google Workspace integration. Can blend internal docs with external research
  Claude     8/10        Native web search with citations launched 2025. Well-reasoned with clear source attribution
  ---------- ----------- ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

**Category 11 --- Personalized AI Assistants**

  --------------------- ----------- -----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  **Tool**              **Score**   **Strength**

  Gemini Gems           7/10        Workspace integration. Newer ecosystem. Less developed templates
  Claude Projects       6/10        Persistent workspaces. Good for long-term context. Not shareable like Custom GPTs

**How to Choose**

-   Brand voice and nuanced writing → Claude.

-   Team in Google Docs/Drive/Gmail → Gemini.

-   Balanced general purpose → ChatGPT.

-   Pick one. Cancel the rest. Save money. Move faster without context switching.
