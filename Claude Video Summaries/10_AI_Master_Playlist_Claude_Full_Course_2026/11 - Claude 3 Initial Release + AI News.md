**Headline --- Claude 3 Launches**

Anthropic announced Claude 3 with claim of being more powerful than ChatGPT. Three versions (the original tier structure):

  ----------- ---------------------------------- -------------------------------------------------------
  **Model**   **Position**                       **Cost**
  Haiku       Least powerful, fast, very cheap   Many times cheaper than Sonnet
  Sonnet      Mid-tier                           Lower than Opus
  Opus        Flagship powerhouse                Highest cost, \~50-60% better performance than Sonnet
  ----------- ---------------------------------- -------------------------------------------------------

**Benchmarks Highlights**

-   Opus outperforms peers on most evaluation benchmarks.

-   Undergraduate-level expert knowledge (MMLU).

-   Graduate-level expert reasoning (GPQA).

-   Basic mathematics.

-   \'Near-human levels of comprehension fluency on complex tasks.\'

-   Even Haiku outperforms GPT-3.5 and Gemini Pro.

-   Sonnet between GPT-3.5 and GPT-4 / Gemini Ultra in many tasks.

**Capability Improvements**

-   Analysis and forecasting.

-   Nuanced content creation.

-   Code generation.

-   Non-English languages --- Spanish, Japanese, French.

-   Vision: Opus better than GPT-4 Vision in many ways, similar to Gemini Ultra in some.

-   Fewer refusals --- better understanding of real harm vs harmless prompts.

-   Improved accuracy of responses.

**Availability**

-   Opus and Sonnet via API in 159 countries.

-   Haiku coming soon at announcement.

**Other AI News in the Same Week**

**Stable Diffusion 3 (Early Preview)**

-   MMDiT architecture (Multimodal Diffusion Transformer) --- same family as Sora.

-   Combines transformer (good at layout) with diffusion (good at detail).

-   Text generation in images --- much better than before but still imperfect (letter spacing, sizing).

-   Outperforms or matches DALL-E 3, Midjourney V6, Ideogram V1 per human evaluators.

-   Not publicly available at the time of recording.

**Microsoft Copilot for Finance**

-   Niche product for finance professionals working with huge spreadsheets.

-   Integrated into Excel, Outlook, Teams.

-   Accesses Dynamics 365, SAP, emails, spreadsheets.

-   Automates variance analysis, collections, invoice matching.

-   Generates insights and recommendations.

-   \'A little dystopian\' --- finance professionals on disappearing jobs list.

**Mistral Large (French Startup)**

-   Promised similar to GPT-4. Ranks \#2 in top LLM list per their metrics.

-   Natively fluent in English, French, Spanish, German, Italian --- nuanced grammar and cultural context.

-   32K token context window.

-   Function calling natively capable.

-   Custom moderation policies.

-   Comparison: close to GPT-4 in common sense, reasoning, knowledge. Outperforms Mixtral and Llama 2 in multilingual.

-   Microsoft partnership promising for European AI.

**Adobe Project Music GenAI Control**

-   Generative AI for crafting and editing custom audio.

-   Generate music from text prompts.

-   Edit using text --- change intensity, extend length, create repeatable loop.

-   Demo done through console commands; no UI yet.

-   Compared to Google Music Effects (drop-down controls), Beethoven (existing tool).

**Amazon Rufus (Shopping Chatbot)**

-   Trained on Amazon catalog and web information.

-   Answer customer questions, recommendations, product comparisons.

-   Washington Post review: not quite an expert. Basic suggestions. Couldn\'t tell why specific products were recommended. Lack of trust.

-   Still under development.

**Wix AI Website Builder**

-   Click \'Create with AI\' → chat with bot about website goals.

-   Get full-fledged website.

-   Themes with different color palettes and looks.

-   Layout changeable via prompt.

-   Real-world results modest --- junior designer level, faster and cheaper.

**GPT Store Updates**

-   Send feedback option to GPT creators.

-   Privacy: hide own email if desired.
